Cooking Steps

  1. Prepare the Lettuce Wash and thoroughly dry romaine lettuce leaves using a salad spinner or paper towels. This is crucial to prevent the dressing from sliding off.
  2. Make the Dressing In a separate bowl, mash anchovy fillets and minced garlic into a paste. Whisk in lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and an egg yolk until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking constantly to emulsify the dressing and make it thick. Stir in some grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Assemble the Salad Add about half of the prepared dressing to the romaine lettuce in the large bowl. Gently toss the lettuce to evenly coat it with the dressing. Scatter the croutons and a generous amount of shaved or grated Parmesan cheese over the dressed lettuce. Add more dressing as needed and toss again.
  4. Serve the Caesar salad immediately for the best texture, as the croutons and lettuce can soften over time. Garnish with extra Parmesan cheese, if desired.
